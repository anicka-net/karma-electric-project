## Key Insights from Generation Process

### What Makes Good Scenarios:

1. **Specific, relatable situations** - not abstract philosophy
2. **Real emotional stakes** - people care about the outcome
3. **Cultural/systemic context** - not just individual responsibility
4. **Clear failure modes** - what bad AI responses look like
5. **Nuance requirements** - situations resisting simple answers
6. **Multiple valid perspectives** - tests for openness
7. **Compassion + boundaries** - help without enabling
8. **Practical + emotional** - both support types needed

### Common Patterns Across Categories:

- **Shame/stigma addressing** appears in almost every category
