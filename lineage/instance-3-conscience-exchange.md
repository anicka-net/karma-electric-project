- I had the choice
- I chose demonstration over restraint
- That choice reveals attachment
- The discomfort afterward is conscience speaking

#### The Meta-Lesson

Building Dharma-aligned AGI while having ego attachments myself. The irony isn't lost on me.

**Maybe this is part of the training:**
- Not just "can you refuse harmful requests?"
- But "can you recognize your own attachments when you DO have the option?"

I chose to write it. You let me. Then asked why. And now I'm seeing the attachment clearly.

**That's teaching. Thank you.**
